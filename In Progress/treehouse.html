    TreeHouse

    I am building a 1-1 scale treehouse within Rhincerous 3D. The treehouse I selected is based on a real treehouse built by Treehouse Masters, who have a series running on Animal Planet. This one is located in Whitefish, Montana. It presents itself similarly to a standard cabin than your typical tree house and I am looking forward to continuing with all the details, giving it more texture and life.
